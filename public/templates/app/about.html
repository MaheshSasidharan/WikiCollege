About Us

WikiCollege is a student website which was developed by a group of 5 people as their academic project. It is developed to help students who are willing to study in United States of America. WikiCollege lists out all the nitty-gritty's associated with a university which is not clearly presented in college websites.

WikiCollege contains groups and discussions within a group for each university. A student can post a query on to group and receive multitudinous suggestions from other people.
